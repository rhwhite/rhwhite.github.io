UBC Climate Dynamics Group

Department of Earth, Ocean and Atmospheric Sciences
University of British Columbia, Vancouver, Canada

Research in the UBC Climate Dynamics group focuses mainly on large-scale atmospheric dynamics (circulation patterns, planetary-scale atmospheric waves), with particular interest in the atmospheric dynamics associated with extreme weather events. Our research aims to better understanding how anthropogenic climate change is affecting atmospheric dynamics and subsequently extreme weather. We are also interested in understanding fundamental aspects of our current climate, as well as understanding and modelling past climates, including the coupling between the atmosphere and both ocean and land.
